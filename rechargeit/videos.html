Plug-in Electric Vehicles 2008

Call for videos: “Why I Want to Drive a Plug-In”

Would you like to drive a plug-in car or truck? Create a video telling us why. If you already own one, create a video showing us how it works. Post your video on YouTube no later than June 4, 2008. We will show some of the most informative, original and funniest videos at the conference.

Videos will be featured on a map on Google.org's RechargeIT website.

If you want to own a plug-in, you could explain:

  • The main reason
  • What difference it would make in your life
  • What difference it would make to the world if we could all buy plug-ins

If you already have a plug-in, you could explain:

  • How it works
  • How it performs (reliability, convenience, etc.)
  • Why you have it
  • What you like about it

Guidelines
The video should be no more than 90 seconds.

How to submit
Craft a video, upload it to YouTube, and then come back and fill out the form below. You’ll need a YouTube account to submit a video. When uploading your video, be sure to select the options to make it public and enable embedding. Reviewed videos will be viewable from the map within a few days, so check back soon.
